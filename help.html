Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the review process take?

Most applications are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Complex cases may take up to 72 hours. You'll be notified via email once your application is processed.

Is there really no cost involved?

Yes, our service is completely free! There are no hidden fees, setup costs, or monthly charges. We believe everyone deserves access to free domain services.

What happens if my application is rejected?

If rejected, you'll receive an email explaining the reason. You can modify your project and reapply. Common reasons include inactive platform links or content that doesn't match the stated purpose.

Can I apply for multiple domains?

Yes, you can submit multiple applications for different projects. Each application is reviewed individually based on its merits and requirements.

What domain extensions do you offer?

We primarily offer subdomains under vinnesia.my.id (e.g., yourname.vinnesia.my.id). This allows us to provide free domains while maintaining quality control.

Do I need technical knowledge to use this service?

No technical expertise required! Our process is designed to be user-friendly. You just need a working website on a supported platform.

How do I update my domain settings after approval?

Once approved, you'll receive detailed instructions via email on how to configure your domain settings and point it to your platform.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

🔐 Can't login with social accounts

  • • Ensure pop-ups are not blocked in your browser
  • • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • • Try using incognito/private browsing mode
  • • Check if you're already logged into the social platform

📋 Form submission errors

  • • Check that all required fields are filled
  • • Ensure your platform URL is valid and accessible
  • • Use a stable internet connection
  • • Try refreshing the page and submitting again

📊 Dashboard not loading applications

  • • Refresh the page using Ctrl+F5 (or Cmd+R on Mac)
  • • Check your internet connection
  • • Make sure you're logged into the same account used for applications
  • • Try logging out and back in

⏱️ Long review times

  • • Standard review takes 24-48 hours
  • • Check your spam folder for notification emails
  • • Complex applications may require additional time
  • • Contact support if it's been over 72 hours
